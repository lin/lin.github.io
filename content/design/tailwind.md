## Tailwind

1. `./bin/bundle add tailwindcss-rails`
1. `./bin/rails tailwindcss:install`
1. `bin/dev`

## class names

1. space-x-1
1. `p, m, w, h, size`
1. `font`: family, weight,
1. `text`: size, align, color
1. `tracking`: letter spacing
1. `rounded`: radius corner
1. `divide`: divider border styling
