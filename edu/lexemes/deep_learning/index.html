    2.1 Scalars, Vectors, Matrices, and Tensors

    1. Identification

    • Which of the following represents a Scalar?
      • A. $5$
      • B. $\vec{v} = [5]$
      • C. $\mathbf{M} = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \end{bmatrix}$
      • D. $(1, 2)$
      • Best Answer: A

    2. Classification

    • The object $\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}$ is best classified as a:
      • A. Scalar
      • B. Vector
      • C. Matrix (Square)
      • D. Tensor (Rank > 2)
      • Best Answer: B

    3. Matrix Recognition

    • Which of the following is a $2 \times 2$ Matrix?
      • A. $[1, 2, 3, 4]$
      • B. $\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{bmatrix}$
      • C. $\begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \end{bmatrix}$
      • D. $10$
      • Best Answer: B

    4. Tensor Hierarchy

    • A tensor of Rank 3 (e.g., dimensions $2 \times 2 \times 2$) is best visualized as:
      • A. A single number
      • B. A row of numbers
      • C. A grid of numbers (like a spreadsheet)
      • D. A cube or block of numbers
      • Best Answer: D
